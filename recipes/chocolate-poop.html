Chocolate Poop Emoji Cookies Recipe

Naughty and Funny Chocolate Poop Emoji Cookies!

Send a little smile with these Poo Emoji Cookies! They are a really delicious chocolate cookie swirled around a Hershey Hug!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp. vanilla extract
  • 3 cups flour
  • 1/2 cup cocoa
  • 1 tsp. baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 bag of Hershey Hugs

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350F degrees.
  2. Cream butter and sugars together in a large mixing bowl.
  3. Add eggs and vanilla and mix well.
  4. In a separate bowl, combine flour, cocoa, baking soda and salt.
  5. Add dry ingredients to wet ingredients and mix well.
  6. Add milk and mix until a soft dough forms.
  7. Unwrap Hershey Hugs.
  8. Take a small ball of dough and wrap it around a Hershey Hug. Roll it in your hands to form a ball.
  9. Place on a parchment-lined baking sheet and bake for 8-10 minutes.
  10. Remove from oven and allow cookies to cool on the baking sheet for 2-3 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.
