Margherita Pizza Recipe

Quick and Easy Margherita Pizza

Quick and easy margherita pizza on homemade crust is your new weeknight go to. A simple, delicious rapid-rise crust is topped with pizza sauce, fresh mozzarella, Italian seasonings, tomatoes, basil, and the best balsamic glaze!

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza crust (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 8 ounces fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 1/2 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 1/4 cup balsamic glaze

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees. Roll out pizza dough on a floured surface to desired thickness. Transfer to a pizza stone or baking sheet.
  2. Spread pizza sauce over dough, leaving a 1-inch border around the edges. Top with mozzarella slices, then sprinkle with Italian seasoning. Arrange cherry tomatoes on top.
  3. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until crust is golden and cheese is melted.
  4. Remove from oven and top with fresh basil leaves. Drizzle with balsamic glaze, then slice and serve.
