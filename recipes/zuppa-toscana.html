Easy Olive Garden Zuppa Toscana Soup

This Easy Olive Garden Zuppa Toscana Soup is a super quick and simple copycat recipe with rich, creamy flavor you can’t resist!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound Italian sausage (I prefer hot, but mild or sweet also work)
  • 1 onion, peeled and diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 6 cups chicken or vegetable stock
  • 1 pound red potatoes, halved and thinly sliced
  • 1 bunch kale, roughly chopped
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup freshly-grated Parmesan cheese
  • fine sea salt and freshly-cracked black pepper
  • optional: crushed red pepper flakes, for extra heat

Steps

  1. Heat a large stockpot over medium-high heat. Add the sausage and cook until browned, crumbling the sausage with a spoon as it cooks. Transfer the sausage to a separate plate using a slotted spoon, and set aside.
  2. Add the onion to the remaining sausage grease in the pan, and sauté for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally. Add the garlic and sauté for 1-2 more minutes, stirring occasionally, until fragrant.
  3. Add the chicken stock and potatoes, and stir to combine. Continue cooking until the soup reaches a simmer. Then reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer for 10 minutes, or until the potatoes are tender.
  4. Stir in the kale, cooked sausage, cream, and Parmesan until combined. Taste and season with salt, pepper, and optional red pepper flakes as needed.
  5. Serve warm, garnished with extra Parmesan cheese if desired.
